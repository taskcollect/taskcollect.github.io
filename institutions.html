Support

Our long-term goal is to work with institutions to provide the necessary support to students and teachers across the world, to make education accessible for everyone. We welcome partnerships with all kinds of educational institutions, including schools, universities, tutoring centres, and unofficial education providers as well. We believe knowledge should belong to everyone, and by reaching out to us, you can let us know how we can make education more accessible for you and others.

Whether you want us to develop platform support for the educational platforms you use, whether you want a feature change to better accommodate your needs, whether you want help with TaskCollect deployment at your institution, or if you just want to discuss something with us, we are happy to discuss your query with you. You can contact us by the info given below:

Contact details

  • Email: ~kvo/taskcollect-discuss@lists.sr.ht
  • IRC: irc.libera.chat/#taskcollect

Please be mindful of the fact that the TaskCollect development team is busy and works on TaskCollect outside of working hours, during our free time. Our response times may reflect this.

Unofficially supported

Unofficially supported institutions are institutions for which the TaskCollect project provides some level of support. However, support for these institutions is not upheld nor necessarily endorsed by the institution. Support is usually provided by volunteers at these institutions.
